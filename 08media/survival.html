Surival

Surviving a zombie apocalypse, or any apocalyptic scenario, is all about careful planning and preparation. The first step is to gather sufficient supplies to survive for 90 days. This includes basic survival items such as water, canned goods, medications, a weapon you know how to use properly, warm clothing, and a carrying bag. In addition to these essentials, you should also store supplies for just getting by. These could include batteries, flashlights, matches, pots and pans, plastic utensils, rope or twine, map, change of clothes, can opener, lighters, camp stove and propane, hatchet or axe, and a first aid book. Lastly, preparing an emergency kit is crucial. This kit should contain adhesive bandages, gauze, medical tape, antibiotics, antiviral drugs, ibuprofen (NSAID), and acetaminophen (Tylenol). Remember that preparation is key when it comes to surviving any disaster scenario. Stay safe!
